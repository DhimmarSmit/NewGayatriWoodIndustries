Use of Pallet

For Warehouse

A pallet is a flat transport structure, which supports goods in a stable fashion while being lifted by a forklift, a pallet jack, a front loader, a jacking device or an erect crane.

For Cargo

Pallets are plane (flat) structures utilised in cargo container ships for supporting goodsor containers. Pallet delivery is the process of moving goods from one place to another on a pallet.
